Fitness Classes

Circuit class

High intensity, high energy training designed to help you tone your body and lose body fat. It involves working on 5-6 exercise stations, performing on each exercise as many times as you can within a set time, with short rest intervals.

Strength class

A class designed to help develop your full body strength. Strength classes uses resistance equipemt, dumbbells, and body weight to help all muscle groups to grow and become stronger.


Body Pump

Body pump is a fast paced, light weight high reps resistance workout. Body pump uses a variety of exercises with the help of fast paced, upbeat music.



Hiit Class

Hiit (which stands for High Intensity Interval training) is a short burst, high intensity workout usually lasting about 30-40 seconds, followed by short period of recovery of around similar time.

Spin Class

A fun cycling workout that focuses on strengh, endurance, high intensity, intervals and recovery. Exercises ranges from sprints, hill climb, time trial and long distance
